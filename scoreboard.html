      📘 Official Ping Pong Rules

      🎯 Starting the Match

      • First server is decided by a random draw (coin toss, etc.).
      • Winner chooses to:
        • Serve
        • Receive
        • Pick table side
      • The other player selects from the remaining options.

      🔁 Serving Order

      • Service changes every 2 points.
      • At 10-10 (deuce), serve alternates after each point until someone leads by 2.

      👥 Doubles Matches

      • Serving team chooses the first server.
      • Receiving team chooses the first receiver.
      • With each service change: receiver becomes server, and previous server’s partner becomes receiver.

      ✅ Legal Serve

      • Ball must be tossed up from a flat palm, at least 6 inches.
      • Contact must be behind the table surface.
      • You cannot hide the ball with your body or arm.
      • If ball hits net on serve, it’s a let and replayed — no penalty or limit.
